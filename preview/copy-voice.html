Playful
Light touches of warmth. A ":)" instead of "😊". Confident but not stiff.
Quirky
Slight unexpectedness. A smiley face at the end. Never weird for weird's sake.
Professional
Clarity first. No jargon. No filler. Every word earns its place.

✕ DON'T — Common mistakes
CTA
Click Here Now!
Imperative + exclamation = pushy. Never.
Empty
No results found for your search query.
Robotic, no personality, no next step
Error
Oops! An unexpected error has occurred. Please try again later.
"Oops" is infantile. "Unexpected" is vague. "Please" is weak.
Label
Click the button below to Submit Your Form
Title Case For Random Words is jarring
Emoji
Great choice! 🎉 Your order is confirmed 🛒✅
Emoji in UI copy = amateur. Use emoticons (:) sparingly instead.
Casing: Sentence case everywhere. Never ALL CAPS except abbreviations (e.g. API, HTML). Never title case for UI copy.
Person: "We" for brand voice. "You" when addressing the user directly. Never "I" for the product.
Length: UI copy max 8 words per line. Descriptions max 2 lines. Tooltips: 1 sentence. Toast: 6 words.
